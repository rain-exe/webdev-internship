Gift Hampers

Started in 2003, Gift Lady is one of South Africa's most established independent gift delivery companies, and online florists.

Wrapping

When it comes to gift-giving, the quality of presentation is essential. Utmost care is taken to ensure that all orders, big or small, are delivered in perfect condition and arrive beautifully gift wrapped with your personalised gift message.

Best Locally Produced Products

Where possible Gift Lady supports South African small businesses. We source the finest products and tailor each order to your specific needs. We also have a wide variety of personalized delivery options.

Online Payments

Shopping online with Gift Lady is safe and secure. Our online payments are facilitated through Paygate and we support all major credit cards like Mastercard, Visa, America Express as well as Paypal.

Nationwide Delivery

We deliver the best quality and most beautifully wrapped hampers, gifts and flowers to just about any destination in south africa.
